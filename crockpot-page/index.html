Crockpot Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 4 Boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Italian Dressing: 1 (16 Ounce) bottle Italian dressing
  • Parmesan Cheese: 1/4 Cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Stir Italian dressing and Parmesan cheese together in a bowl
  2. Place chicken in a slow cooker, and pour dressing mixture over chicken. Cover and cook on low until chicken is tender, no longer pink in the center, and the jucies run clear, about 8 hours.
