Salmon

Ingredients

  • Sesame oil: Teriyaki marinade for salmon starts with sesame oil.
  • Lemon juice: Adds welcome freshness and brightness.
  • Soy sauce: Lends desired savory flavor.
  • Brown sugar: Gives the marinate pleasant sweetness.
  • Sesame seeds: A tablespoon of sesame seeds will add mild flavor and crunch.
  • Spices: You'll need ground mustard, ground ginger, and garlic powder.
  • Salmon: This recipe make four salmon steaks.

Steps

  1. Make the marinate on the stove, then set aside 1/4 cup for basting.
  2. Marinate the salmon in zip-top bags in the refrigerator for up to two hours.
  3. Broil the salmon until it flakes easily, brushing with the reserved marinade as-needed.
