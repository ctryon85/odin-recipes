Crockpot Italian Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 (16ounce) bottle Italian dressing (such as Olive Garden Signature Italian Dressing)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts

Directions

Step 1

  1. Stir Italian dressing and Parmesan cheese together in a bowl

Step 2

  1. Place chicken in a slow cooker
  2. Pour dressing mixture of chicken
  3. Cover and cook on low until chicken is tender
  4. No longer pink in the center, and juices run clear
  5. About 8 hours
